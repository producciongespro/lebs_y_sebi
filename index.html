Resource Site for Experimental Bilingual High Schools and Bilingual Sections Spanish-English

The goal of the site is to provide support and guidance to teachers for the mediation of the goals of the New English Syllabus and development of communicative competence. Also make the teaching and learning process more related to the needs of the real world. This presents tasks for each competition and becomes a rich source of ideas that provide useful information for the development of Oral and Written Comprehension and Production.
